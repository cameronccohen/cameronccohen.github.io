Projects and experience

My thesis used a 1.6b observation dataset of individual voting records to study how changes in electoral composition influence the midterm loss cycle (with Profs. Andrei Shleifer and Vincent Pons).

In school, I wrote papers/final projects on methods for deep learning generalization in the context of adherence to Tuberculosis treatment and using classifiers to incentivize strategic effort allocation under loss aversion.

Previously, I made an iOS drawing app that reached top 31 on the App Store (iSketch), designed and built the iOS app for TOMS Shoes, and made a fun puzzle game.
